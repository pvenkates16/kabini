Welcome to Hawabaaz Getaway

Escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse yourself in the serene wilderness of Kabini. At Hawabaaz Getaway, we offer an unparalleled wildlife experience, blending adventure with comfort.

Our expert naturalists will guide you through thrilling safaris, where you can spot majestic tigers, elusive leopards, herds of elephants, and a myriad of bird species. Discover the untouched beauty of nature with us.

Book your adventure today and create memories that will last a lifetime!

About Hawabaaz

Founded with a passion for wildlife and conservation, Hawabaaz Getaway is dedicated to providing responsible and enriching eco-tourism experiences. We believe in sustainable practices that protect the natural habitat and support local communities.

Our team comprises experienced professionals who are deeply knowledgeable about Kabini's flora and fauna. We are committed to ensuring your safety and providing an educational journey into the heart of the wilderness.
