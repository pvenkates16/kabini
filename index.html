Welcome to Kabini!

Nestled on the banks of the Kabini River, this enchanting destination in Karnataka, India, is a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. Known for its lush forests, diverse fauna, and serene backwaters, Kabini offers an unforgettable experience away from the bustling city life. Prepare to be mesmerized by its untouched beauty and the thrill of spotting majestic creatures in their natural habitat.

Experience Highlights

Wildlife Safaris

Embark on thrilling jeep safaris and boat safaris to witness the majestic wildlife, including tigers, leopards, elephants, and a plethora of bird species.

Nature Walks

Explore the serene trails around your resort with expert naturalists, discovering the smaller wonders of the forest.

Boat Rides on Kabini River

Enjoy peaceful boat rides, offering unique perspectives of the riverside wildlife and the stunning landscapes, especially at sunset.

Local Culture & Cuisine

Immerse yourself in the local culture, interact with the indigenous communities, and savor authentic South Indian delicacies.
